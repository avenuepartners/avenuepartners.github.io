Services

The following are examples of the research and consulting services Christine provides.

  • Research and statistics consulting for corporate clients (e.g. designing employee engagement surveys or focus group protocols; analyzing quantitative data in SPSS, qualitative data in NVIVO; writing reports)
  • Research and statistics consulting for academic projects (e.g. cleaning data, creating variables, running descriptives, and conducting multivariate analyses in preparation of academic journal articles)
  • Speaking engagements and diversity & inclusion brainstorming and strategy sessions
  • Dissertation editing and consulting (refining topics, proposal and dissertation content editing, guidance on methodology and analysis)
  • Writing on the advancement of women and diversity and inclusion more broadly (e.g. blogs, articles, business case documents)
  • Media interviews; commentary on research
